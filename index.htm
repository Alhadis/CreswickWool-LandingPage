Creswick Woollen Mills

Creswick Woollen Mills is the home of luxurious, natural fibre products designed in Australia. Founded in 1947 Creswick Woollen Mills is now the only coloured woollen spinning mill of its kind in Australia.

Creswick has six stores across Victoria, Australia and stocks many of its products in the famous Australian department store David Jones.

About

Located in Creswick, a small country township approximately 120km from Melbourne, only 20 minutes north-east of Ballarat and 20 minutes south-east of Daylesford, Creswick Woollen Mills has become a household name both in Australia and globally. Creswick's continued success is attributed to ongoing research and innovation, as well as dedication to high quality with exclusive designs.
